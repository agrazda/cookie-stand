PAT'S COOKIE STAND

Welcome to our home page! We are so glad you are here! Pat has developed a recipe for a coffee-time confection called The Salmon Cookie. These cookies are made into the shape of a salmon and has just a hint of fishy goodness.

When you come by the store dont foget to pick up a Cookie Stand T-shirt, or even a salmon keychain!

Pat and his family have worked for years perfecting the Salmon Cookie. They wanted to be able to give people somthing that had never been done.

This is a true family business with all members involved. Pat's two children created the look of the cookie. Pat's wife handles all of their merchandise and design. And Pat works tirlessly trying to develope new flavors for his customers to enjoy
